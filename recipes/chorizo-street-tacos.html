Easy Chorizo Street Tacos

Description

Bring the delicious taste of street food home with these delicious and simple Chorizo Street Tacos. With only a short list of ingredients, you can get a whole lot of flavour, without breaking the bank.

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo sausage link (remove casing and crumble meat)
  • 2 tbsp chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Mix chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce together in a bowl
  2. Heat a skillet over med-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp (5-7 mins). Transfer chorizo to a plate , leaving grease in the skillet
  3. Heat tortillas in the leftover grease in the skillet over med-high heat until warm (1-2mins per side).
  4. Stack 2 tortillas on top of each other for each taco. Fill with chorizo, onion, and cliantro.
  5. Enjoy!
