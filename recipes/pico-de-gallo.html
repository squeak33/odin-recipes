Pico De Gallo

Description

Enjoy this deliciously fresh topping on tacos, nachos, or eat it with tortilla chips. It's so bright and fresh, your guests will swoon.

Ingredients

  • 6 roma tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 red onion (minced)
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 jalapeno pepper (minced)
  • 1/2 lime (juiced)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 pinch cumin
  • salt & black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Stir the tomatoes, cilantro, onion, jalapeno, lime juice, garlic, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Refrigerate for at least 3 hourds before serving.
  3. Serve & enjoy!
