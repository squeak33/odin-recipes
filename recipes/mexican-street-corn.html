Mexican Street Corn

Description

Add some flavour to your corn on the cob and kick it up a notch with this Mexican twist. Guaranteed to please all the spice lovers in your life, but be sure to have some dental floss handy for after the meal!

Ingredients

  • 6 ears of corn (unhusked)
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup crumbled Cojita cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 limes (juiced)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees (F)
  2. Place corn (in husks) directly onto the oven rack
  3. Roast in the preheatd oven until tender and cooked through (40-45min)
  4. Peel down husks and rub each ear of corn with 1 tbsp of butter.
  5. Sprinkle each with Cojita cheese, cilantro, chili powder, and lime juice.
  6. Serve, and Enjoy!
